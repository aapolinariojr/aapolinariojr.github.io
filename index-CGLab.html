Antonio Apolinário is an assistant professor at the Computer Institute of the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA). He obtained a PhD in Systems and Computer Engineering with emphasis on Computer Graphics at PESC/COPPE , Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), Brazil. His research interests are on Computer Graphics, with emphasis on 3D Modeling, Real-Time Rendering, Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality.
